Our Story

Building Worlds from Scratch

FunLogic Studio is a passion-fueled indie game development studio. We believe the best games come from genuine creativity and relentless curiosity ... not multi-million dollar budgets.

Founded by two dreamers united by a love for games, we build everything from scratch: 3D adventures, horror experiences, and educational platforms that actually engage students. Every pixel, polygon, and sound is crafted in-house.

Our engine of choice currently is Godot 4 ... open source, powerful, and the perfect fit for a team building tomorrow's games today. We're not just a studio. We're a movement.
